InfinityStoneCollector!

How to play:

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are 4 infinity stones below. By clicking on a stone, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number displayed below. You lose the game if your total score goes above said number.

The value of each stone is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each stone. Have fun!
